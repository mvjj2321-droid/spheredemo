Signal path · local & streaming end-to-end

The Sphere voice pipeline

Audio in, audio out, nothing leaves the machine. Every stage streams into the next — no waiting for a full sentence, a full reply, or a full clip.

01 Frontend / Browser UI

sphere.html

Opens the mic, renders the orb, and streams tokens and audio back to the person in real time.

HTTP / WebSocket ↕
02 FastAPI / Flask backend

app.py

Owns the socket connection and routes audio and text between the browser and every model below.

03 Input device

Microphone

Raw audio captured straight from the browser, no intermediate file.

04 Speech → text

Whisper STT

Transcribes in a rolling stream, word by word, as you speak.

05 Reasoning

Ollama LLM

Local model, generating and streaming its reply token by token.

06 Text → speech

Kokoro TTS

Turns each finished phrase into audio as soon as it's ready — no waiting on the full response.

07 Output device

Speaker

Plays the synthesized audio as it streams in, no full-clip buffering.

08 Visualization

sphere.html — orb

The browser reacts to the audio in flight, driving the orb's motion, then loops the connection back to step 01.
